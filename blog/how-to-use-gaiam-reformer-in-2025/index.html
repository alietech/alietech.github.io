1. The Hundred

  • Purpose: Builds core strength and stability.
  • Steps: Lie on the reformer carriage. With your legs in a tabletop position and your arms stretched alongside your body, pump your arms up and down while inhaling and exhaling in sets of ten.

2. Leg Circles

  • Purpose: Enhances lower body flexibility and strength.
  • Steps: Attach the straps to your feet. Keep your legs extended upwards and form gentle circles, switching directions halfway through the set.

3. Long Stretch

  • Purpose: Strengthens upper body and improves posture.
  • Steps: Begin in a plank position with hands on the foot bar. Extend your body backwards and forward, keeping the core engaged and back straight.
