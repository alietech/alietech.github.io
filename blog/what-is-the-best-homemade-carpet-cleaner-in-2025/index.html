Top 3 Homemade Carpet Cleaner Recipes for 2025

1. Baking Soda and Vinegar Miracle Cleaner

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of baking soda
  • 1 cup of distilled white vinegar
  • 2 cups of warm water
  • 20 drops of essential oil (such as lavender or tea tree)

Instructions:

  1. Sprinkle baking soda generously over the carpet.
  2. Mix vinegar and essential oil in a spray bottle.
  3. Spray the vinegar mixture over the baking soda.
  4. Let it fizz and bubble for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Gently scrub the area with a soft brush and blot with a clean cloth.

2. Lemon and Salt Refreshing Scrub

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of coarse salt
  • 1/4 cup of lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup of distilled water

Instructions:

  1. Mix salt, lemon juice, and distilled water in a bowl.
  2. Apply the mixture to the stained area.
  3. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  4. Scrub with a damp brush and wipe clean.

3. Essential Oil and Baking Soda Freshener

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of baking soda
  • 15 drops of essential oil (eucalyptus or peppermint)

Instructions:

  1. Combine baking soda and essential oil in a jar.
  2. Sprinkle the mixture over your carpet and let it sit for 15 minutes.
  3. Vacuum thoroughly for a fresh and clean carpet.
