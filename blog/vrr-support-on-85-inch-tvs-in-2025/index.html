Variable Refresh Rate (VRR) is a technology designed to enhance the gaming experience. It allows your TV to refresh its image at the same rate as the graphics card of your gaming console or PC. This synchronization minimizes annoying screen tearing and stuttering, delivering a smoother and more responsive gaming experience. As video games continue to push the boundaries of graphics and performance, VRR has become essential for gamers.
