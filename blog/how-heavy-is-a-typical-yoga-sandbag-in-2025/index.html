As of 2025, a typical yoga sandbag weighs between 7 to 10 pounds (approximately 3.2 to 4.5 kilograms). These sandbags are usually made from durable fabrics such as cotton or canvas, ensuring longevity and ease of use.

Why the Weight Matters

Selecting the right weight for a yoga sandbag is crucial, as it determines the effectiveness of its usage:

  • Supports Postures: A correctly weighted sandbag can provide gentle pressure, aiding in the relaxation and deepening of postures.
  • Enhances Stretches: By placing sandbags on strategic parts of the body, practitioners can experience more profound and effective stretches.
  • Improves Stability: The added weight can help stabilize poses that require balance or alignment, especially in restorative practices.
